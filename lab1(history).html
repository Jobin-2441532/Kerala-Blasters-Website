OUR PAST PERFORMANCES







Kerala Blasters FC, one of the most supported teams in the Indian Super League (ISL), has had a mixed performance history since the league's inception in 2014. Known for their passionate fanbase, the "Manjappada," the team has reached the ISL finals three times but has yet to secure the trophy.

In the inaugural season (2014), Kerala Blasters made an impressive run to the finals but lost narrowly to ATK. Two years later, they reached the final again but suffered another heartbreaking loss to ATK in a penalty shootout. Despite their strong start to the league, inconsistency plagued the team in subsequent seasons. They often struggled to qualify for the playoffs, with notable challenges in defensive transitions and away form.

However, their title drought continued with a loss to Hyderabad FC in a penalty shootout. In the following season (2022-23), the team maintained a strong home record but faltered in away games and defensive consistency, exiting in the playoffs. Despite these challenges, Kerala Blasters have left a lasting impact on Indian football with iconic moments, including their record-breaking attendance and contributions to player development, such as Sahal Abdul Samad and Sandesh Jhingan.

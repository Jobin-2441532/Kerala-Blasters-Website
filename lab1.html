KERALA BLASTERS



Kerala Blasters FC is one of the most popular football clubs in India, competing in the Indian Super League (ISL). Founded in 2014 and based in Kochi, Kerala, the team is known for its passionate fanbase, nicknamed the "Manjappada," which translates to "Yellow Brigade." This vibrant fan group has set benchmarks for football fandom in the country, often filling the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium with electrifying energy and unwavering support.The team's identity is rooted in Kerala’s rich football culture, symbolized by its vibrant yellow kit. Over the years, Kerala Blasters have produced memorable moments in the ISL, reaching the finals multiple times. The club is also focused on nurturing local talent, contributing to the development of Indian football. Despite challenges on the pitch, Kerala Blasters remains a beloved team, epitomizing resilience, unity, and a love for the beautiful game.

ABOUT CLUB


Kerala Blasters FC, established in 2014, is one of the founding clubs of the Indian Super League (ISL) and a symbol of Kerala's deep-rooted passion for football. The team was co-founded by cricketing legend Sachin Tendulkar, whose involvement brought significant attention to the club in its formative years. The name "Blasters" is a tribute to Tendulkar’s explosive batting style, and the club’s vibrant yellow colors represent the state’s cultural and traditional identity. The Blasters played their inaugural ISL match on October 13, 2014, against NorthEast United FC. Despite a slow start, the team reached the final in their debut season but lost to Atlético de Kolkata. This set the tone for an enduring rivalry with Kolkata-based teams. The Blasters again reached the ISL finals in 2016 and 2022, but a title has remained elusive, making them one of the most passionately supported yet luckless teams in the league.Known for their electrifying fanbase, the "Manjappada," Kerala Blasters enjoy unparalleled home support, with the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in Kochi often filled to capacity. Off the pitch, the club has invested heavily in grassroots development, contributing to the growth of football in Kerala and fostering young talent across India.
